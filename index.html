The Well

The Well is a place just for High Schoolers to go after school. It has the perfect mix of study space and fun. Whether you're planning on studying, playing games like basketball and ping pong, or grabbing a drink (like the Galaxy Energy Spritzer) from The Well's coffee bar, it's got you covered. Strike the right balance between Work and Play at The Well!

This Months Featured Items
It Is Well

It Is Well, is a vibrant concoction that is a refreshing blue energy spritzer designed to invigorate your senses. Infused with the electrifying essence of blue energy, this delightful beverage combines the crisp notes of green apple, the tangy allure of blue raspberry, and the zesty kick of lemonade. With its sweet and energizing blend, It Is Well is the perfect companion to keep you revitalized throughout the day.

Birthday Cake Ice Cream

Indulge in the celebration of flavor with our Birthday Cake Ice Cream. This delightful treat boasts a delicious medley of birthday cake flavors, ensuring every bite is a party in your mouth. Its soft and velvety texture allows for swift enjoyment, encouraging you to savor the moment or, better yet, order another round of this festive delight.

Cherry Cream Soda

Satisfy your taste buds with our Cherry Cream Soda, a tantalizing blend of Sprite, Cherry, and Vanilla, crowned with a half-and-half whipped cream topper. The finishing touch, a delightful peach ring on top, adds a touch of sweetness to every sip. Whether you prefer to enjoy it as a refreshing standalone drink or pair it with a delightful snack, the choice is yours to savor.
